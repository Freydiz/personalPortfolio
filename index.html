About Me

Hi! My name is Henriette Riis Steinbach. I am an enthusiastic developer who loves to create useful and intuitive applications and interfaces. I have experience working with user-centered design and how to include the user in the development process. I can develop wire-frames at varying levels of fidelity and enjoy prototyping ideas. My objective is to succeed as a creative designer and developer dedicated to building and optimizing performance on user-centric applications. I expect a steep learning curve to get there.
